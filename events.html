———《RESPAWN · 復活》

故事講述一個人意外「進入」了福音事件，親身經歷耶穌所做的——醫治、赦免、憐憫，然後一步步走向十字架的抉擇。透過這個故事，讓我們重新思考耶穌的愛與十字架的意義。

The story tells of a person who accidentally "enters" into the Gospel event, experiences what Jesus did — healing, forgiveness, mercy — and then gradually walks to the choice of the cross. Through this story, let us rethink the love of Jesus and the meaning of the cross.

受難節福音話劇「復活」

✝️ Good Friday Gospel Play
"Resurrection"

4月3日 晚上7時 · April 3, 7PM

你有沒有想過，如果你突然「進入」了一個福音故事，看見耶穌伸手醫治並對你說「你的罪赦免了」，你會怎麼選擇？我們邀請你踏上這段無法置身事外的旅程，重新發現愛、犧牲與重生。4月3日於教會隆重獻演。

Have you ever wondered what would happen if you suddenly "entered" a gospel story? If you see Jesus reach out to heal and hear Him say, "Your sins are forgiven," what would you choose? We invite you to embark on this journey — to rediscover love, sacrifice, and rebirth. Grandly performed at church on April 3rd.
