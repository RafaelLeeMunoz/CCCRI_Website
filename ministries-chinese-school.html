中文學校

Chinese School

羅德島華人基督教會附屬中文學校創立至今已逾四十二年，旨在讓北美華人家庭的下一代學習中文、傳承中華文化。自創校以來，學校不僅培育了眾多美籍華裔的下一代，更為許多非華語學生提供了學習中文、認識中華文化的機會。

The Rhode Island Chinese Christian Church Affiliated Chinese School was established forty-two years ago with the aim of enabling the next generation of Chinese families in North America to learn Chinese and inherit Chinese culture. In the more than forty years since its founding, the school has not only nurtured many of the next generation of Chinese Americans but has also provided an opportunity for many non-Chinese-speaking students to learn Chinese and understand Chinese culture.

我們的教學方式是採用多種生動、體驗式的方法，在有限的教學時間內充分激發學生對中華文化的興趣，使他們產生學習了解中華文化最有用工具——普通話——的渴望。

Our teaching method is to use a variety of lively and experiential approaches to fully stimulate students' interest in Chinese culture within a limited teaching time, so that they can have a desire to learn the most useful tool for understanding Chinese culture – Mandarin Chinese.

我們的老師以無限的愛心、豐富的經驗和無私的奉獻精神教導學生。

Our teachers teach our students with boundless love, rich experience, and selfless dedication.

我們的教學主要使用普通話及簡體中文，同時亦經常為學生提供接觸繁體中文的機會。

Our teaching primarily uses Mandarin and Simplified Chinese. We also frequently provide students with opportunities to engage with Traditional Chinese.

🕐 上課時間 Class Time

每逢主日下午1:45，於羅德島華人教會舉行，春、秋兩個學期。
Every Sunday afternoon at 1:45 at the Rhode Island Chinese Church, during the spring and fall semesters.
