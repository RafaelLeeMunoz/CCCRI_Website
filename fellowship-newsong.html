新歌團契

New Song Fellowship

「你們要向耶和華唱新歌，全地都要向耶和華歌唱。」

[Psalm 96:1] Sing a new song to the Lord, and all the earth sing to the Lord.

「教養孩童，使他走當行的道，就是到老他也不偏離。」

[Proverbs 22:6] Train up a child in the way he should go; even when he is old he will not depart from it.

— 詩篇 Psalm 96:1  |  箴言 Proverbs 22:6

新歌團契是一個以年輕家庭為主的弟兄姊妹群體，大家在神的話語中一同建立、服事、成長。我們歡迎所有弟兄姊妹及慕道友加入我們的團契聚會。目前大部分成員在羅德島或麻薩諸塞州工作。我們也有教會的資深成員參與並帶領團契和聚會，給予靈性和聖經上的支持與陪伴。除了定期的查經小組外，我們也不定期舉行聚會和郊遊活動。

New Song Fellowship is a community of brothers and sisters, primarily from young families, who build, serve, and grow together in the Word of God. We welcome all brothers, sisters, and seekers to join our fellowship meetings. Most of our current members work in Rhode Island or Massachusetts. We also have senior members from our church who participate in and lead our fellowship and meetings, providing spiritual and biblical support and fellowship. In addition to regular Bible study groups, we hold occasional meetings and outings.

♥ 聚會時間：每月第一及第三個主日下午兩點 ♥

♥ Meeting Time: 2 PM on the first and third Sundays of each month ♥
