迦勒團契

Caleb Fellowship

To know the truth, to pursue spirituality, to witness to the faith, to be healthy in body and mind, to live a joyful life, to have good interpersonal relationships, and to support and help one another.

Seniors aged 65 and over, Christians, and anyone interested in Christianity are welcome to attend our meetings. We have prepared the following exciting activities:

  1. Fellowship meetings are held at 10:00 AM on the second Saturday of each month in church or online, and include hymns, short sermons, testimonies, and special topics.
  2. A sports class is held online every Wednesday at 11:00 AM.
  3. There are occasional outings held throughout the year.
