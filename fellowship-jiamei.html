家美團契

Jia Mei Fellowship

團契命名為「家美」，顧名思義，表達了對「家庭幸福」的期盼，同時也寓意我們都在美麗的「美國」這片土地上共同為家。

The fellowship is named "Jia Mei," which, as the name suggests, expresses the hope for "family happiness" and also implies that we are all home in the "beautiful land" of America.

目前，我們約有四十戶會員家庭，主要以普通話交流。讚美、禱告、查經、傳福音、守望相助，彼此關懷，是我們團契的核心活動。家美每月舉行一次全家福聯誼聚會，另有每兩週一次的小組查經，分為「活水查經組」和「天路歷程組」。此外，家美還提供多樣化的大家庭活動，如農曆新年慶典和郊遊，促進家庭間與神的共融，建立和睦的關係，增進家庭幸福。歡迎您加入我們。

Currently, we have approximately 40 member families, primarily speaking Mandarin. Praise, prayer, Bible study, evangelism, mutual support, and care for one another are the core activities of our fellowship. JiaMei has a monthly whole-family fellowship meeting, as well as twice-monthly group Bible studies, divided into the Living Water Bible Study Group and the Pilgrim's Journey Group. In addition, JiaMei offers a variety of extended family activities, such as Chinese New Year celebrations and outings, to promote communion with God among families, build harmonious relationships, and enhance family happiness. We welcome you to join us.

♥ 聚會時間：每月第三個星期五，晚上 7:30 – 9:30，提供愛筵。♥

♥ Party Time: The third Friday of every month, 7:30 PM – 9:30 PM, with a love feast provided. ♥
