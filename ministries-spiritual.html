靈命與造就

Spiritual Life & Edification

教會主日學是教導聖經基要真理的課堂，幫助信徒認識神、認識信仰，是弟兄姊妹學習真理、接受裝備與培訓的地方。教會主日學自1996年開始，經過數十年的摸索，已發展出一套較為完整的教學體系。

Church Sunday school is a classroom that teaches fundamental truths of the Bible, helps believers know God and their faith, and is a place for brothers and sisters to learn the truth and receive equipping and training. Church Sunday school began in 1996, and after decades of exploration, it has developed a relatively complete teaching system.

教會主日學仿照大學學期制，每年分為春、夏、秋、冬四個學季。春、秋學季各有12堂課（3個月），夏、冬學季各有6堂課（1.5個月）。春、秋學季分別以普通話和廣東話授課，夏、冬學季則以普通話合班授課。

Similar to a university semester, church Sunday school is divided into four semesters each year: spring, summer, autumn, and winter. The spring and autumn semesters each have 12 classes (3 months), while the summer and winter semesters each have 6 classes (1.5 months). Classes in the spring and autumn semesters are taught separately in Mandarin and Cantonese, while classes in the summer and winter semesters are taught jointly in Mandarin.

春、秋主日學按信徒的屬靈歷程，分設福音班、受浸班、門徒班、聖經縱覽班及單卷聖經班。近年來，因應會眾需要，亦開辦了系統神學班及長者讀經班。夏、冬主日學則以實用課程為主，如基督徒家庭生活及職場視角等，每季主題各有不同。

The spring and autumn Sunday school sessions are divided into Gospel, Baptism, Discipleship, Bible Overview, and Single Bible Book classes, based on the believers' spiritual journey. In recent years, in response to the needs of the congregation, systematic theology classes and Bible reading classes for the elderly have also been offered. The summer and winter Sunday school sessions focus on practical courses, such as Christian family life and workplace perspectives, with different themes each season.

每年約有20位主日學老師忠心服事於教導崗位，在造就學生的同時，自己也深受造就和祝福。願弟兄姊妹積極參與主日學事工——不做學生，便做老師——實現教會多年前訂立的美好目標！

Each year, approximately 20 Sunday school teachers diligently serve in their teaching positions, edifying the students while also receiving profound edification and blessings themselves. May brothers and sisters actively participate in the ministry of Sunday school—if not students, then teachers—to realize the beautiful goals the church set many years ago!

聖經單卷班

雅歌

 春季粵語主日學將採用中神「生命之道」課程《雅歌》。日期2026/1/19日至3/22日的主日早晨9:45–11:00。

《雅歌》是聖經的「詩歌智慧書」之一，是不容易解讀的一卷書，除了婚禮，都甚少用到它。其內容用語較深，意象豐富，運用典故。聖經嘗試透過無數意象來理解存在於神與人之間這個關係的奧秘。這卷書就是透過新郎、丈夫與妻子這個意象，以男女愛情與婚姻來呈現上主與子民的關係，一個「像極了愛情」的關係，讓我們從中去學習如何彼此相愛和如何去愛神。		 粵
