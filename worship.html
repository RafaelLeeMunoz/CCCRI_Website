中文崇拜

Chinese Service

主日 Sunday 11:15 AM – 12:50 PM

以普通話和廣東話進行的崇拜，透過聖經和讚美詩聯結我們的華語會眾。

A worship experience in Mandarin and Cantonese, connecting our Chinese-speaking congregation through Scripture and praise.

崇拜程序

Worship Order · Rhode Island Chinese Christian Church

11:15 召集 Called
11:17 詩歌敬拜（含詩班） Song worship (incl. choir)
11:32 禱告 Prayer
11:37 讀經 Bible reading
11:40 講道 Preaching the Word
12:35 回應詩 Response to Poetry
12:38 奉獻禱告 Offering prayer
12:40 報告 Announcements
12:46 祝福 Blessing
12:47 三一頌 Trinity Praise
12:50 聚會結束 Service ends
13:00 愛宴開始（餐廳） Fellowship lunch begins (dining hall)

英文崇拜

English Service

主日 Sunday 10:00 AM – 11:10 AM

英語崇拜，歡迎第二代及所有英語會眾參加。

An English-language worship service for our second-generation and English-speaking congregation members.

City Light CCCRI 是我們充滿活力的英語會眾，歡迎學生、年輕專業人士和家庭加入，體驗當代敬拜、相關教導和真實的社群生活。

City Light CCCRI is our vibrant English congregation, welcoming students, young professionals, and families. Connect with us for contemporary worship, relevant teaching, and authentic community.

主日程序

Sunday Schedule · City Light CCCRI

10:00 WORSHIP SERVICE

來與我們一起敬拜，讚美感謝神，尋求認識創造我們之神的愛。
Come worship with us as we give praises and thanksgiving to our God and seek to understand the love of the God who created us.

11:20 COFFEE CHAT

與我們一起喝咖啡、吃甜甜圈和鬆餅！在咖啡和點心中增進彼此認識。
Fellowship with us over coffee, donuts, muffins and more! Let us get to know one another as we chat over coffee and snacks.

11:45 SUNDAY SCHOOL

深入探索聖經，學習如何將聖經應用於日常生活中。
Dwell deeper into the scriptures and navigate the intricacies of the Bible as we seek to apply it into our daily lives.

1:00 LUNCH FELLOWSHIP

來享用美味的家常便飯，認識 CityLight 的弟兄姊妹。第一次來的訪客，午餐我們請客！
Join us for a delicious home cooked meal and get to know the people of CityLight. First time visitors, lunch is on us!
