佛蒙特州秋日赏枫之旅Vermont Fall Foliage Trips

家庭团契佛蒙特州赏枫之旅（十月十至十三日）Home Fellowship Vermont Fall Foliage Tour (Oct. 10–13)

Contributed by Xiong Fang, editor-in-chief of the church website.

十月十至十三日，家庭团契逾四十名弟兄姐妹前往佛蒙特州，入住威利斯顿的Sonnets ES Suites，进行为期四天的赏枫之旅。此行不仅让众人欣赏壮美的秋色，也提供了在团契与敬拜中经历主丰盛恩典的机会。From October 10th to 13th, over forty brothers and sisters from Home Fellowship traveled to Vermont for a four-day fall foliage viewing trip, staying at Sonnets ES Suites in Williston, VT. The trip not only allowed them to appreciate the magnificent autumn scenery but also provided an opportunity to experience the abundant grace of the Lord through fellowship and worship.

星期六，众人前往佛蒙特州夏洛特市的费罗山州立公园远足，全程约1.9英里。途中，枫林层层叠叠，微风拂面。登顶后俯瞰查普莱恩湖与山间秋色，众人赞叹神创造的奇妙与伟大。On Saturday, everyone went hiking in Mount Philo State Park in Charlotte, VT — a trail of about 1.9 miles. 「愿你的旨意成就」——年终回顾"May Your Will Be Done" — Year-End Reflection

Scripture: Matthew 6:9–13

教会2023年主题为「愿你的旨意成就」。全年每主日讲道均围绕此主题。年终之际，正宜回顾神借每个月份向我们的呼召：The theme of our church in 2023 was "May Your will be done." Every Sunday's sermon throughout the year revolved around this theme. As the year closes, it is fitting to reflect on what God has called us to through each month:

一月——相交；二月——爱众人；三月——敬拜祂；四月——作祂的门徒；五月——服侍；六月——顺服；七月——献上自己；八月——成为祂的儿女；九月——持守信仰；十月——传福音；十一月——被装备；十二月——与祂同在天国。January — fellowship; February — love everyone; March — worship Him; April — be His disciples; May — serve; June — obey; July — dedicate ourselves; August — become His children; September — uphold our faith; October — share the gospel; November — be equipped; December — be with Him in Heaven.

A. 认识神的旨意A. Understanding God's Will

圣经中共有七十四处经文与「神的旨意」相关——旧约十六处，新约五十八处。神按自己的旨意行事（但4:35）；祂的旨意无可阻挡（罗9:19），永不改变（来6:17）。祂在万世以前预定了自己的旨意（弗2:11），万物皆按祂旨意被造（启4:11）。基督按父的旨意舍己，救赎罪人（加1:4）。There are 74 Bible verses related to "God's will" — 16 in the Old Testament and 58 in the New Testament. God acts according to His own will (Dan 4:35); His will is irresistible (Rom 9:19) and unchangeable (Heb 6:17). He ordained His will before the ages (Eph 2:11), and all things were created by His will (Rev 4:11). Christ sacrificed Himself according to the Father's will to redeem the sinful world (Gal 1:4).

B. 神旨意的要求B. The Requirements of God's Will

我们蒙召祷告「愿你的旨意成就」（太6:10），甘心遵行神的旨意（诗40:8），明白主的旨意（弗5:17），从心里服侍（弗6:6），并凡事谢恩（帖前5:18）。神的旨意是要人成圣、行善，并甘心乐意地牧养（彼前5:2）。We are called to pray "May your will be done" (Matt 6:10), to willingly do God's will (Ps 40:8), to understand the Lord's will (Eph 5:17), to serve from the heart (Eph 6:6), and to give thanks in all circumstances (1 Thess 5:18). God's will is for people to be sanctified, to do good, and to shepherd willingly and joyfully (1 Pet 5:2).

C. 认识神旨意的功效C. The Efficacy of Understanding God's Will

神垂听遵行祂旨意之人的祷告（约9:31）。爱神的人，万事都互相效力（罗8:28）。神按祂旨意预定人得为业（弗1:11）。遵行神旨意的，永远长存（约壹2:17），惟有遵行天父旨意的，才能进天国（太7:21）。God hears the prayers of those who obey His will (John 9:31). In all things, God works together for good to those who love Him (Rom 8:28). God has predestined people to be an inheritance according to His will (Eph 1:11). Whoever obeys God's will will live forever (1 John 2:17), and only by doing His will can one enter the Kingdom of Heaven (Matt 7:21).

D. 为何有时错失神的旨意D. Why God's Will Is Sometimes Missed

人可以用无知的言语遮蔽神的旨意（伯38:2）；有人藐视它（诗107:11）；有人以己见拒绝它（路7:30）；也有人抗拒或扣留它（诗2:2；徒20:27）。Man can obscure God's will with ignorant words (Job 38:2); some despise it (Ps 107:11); others reject it for their own opinions (Luke 7:30); still others resist or withhold it (Ps 2:2; Acts 20:27).

E. 如何寻求神的旨意E. How to Understand God's Will

Be instructed in the Word (Rom 2:18). Offer your body as a living sacrifice, renewing your mind (Rom 12:1–2): "Do not conform to the pattern of this world, but be transformed by the renewing of your mind. Then you will be able to test and approve what God's will is — his good, pleasing and perfect will." Resolve to do His will (John 7:17). Be filled with the Holy Spirit (Eph 5:17–18).
