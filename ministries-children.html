Welcome to Lighthouse Children's Ministry

The Lighthouse Kids program aims to create a fun and safe space for children to learn about Christ and His character. We serve children from birth to sixth grade. Our children's worship service is held every Sunday morning at 11:15 AM at the church. You are welcome to join us on Sundays to learn about the Bible and the teachings of Jesus Christ!

Our desire is for children to experience God through a Christ-centered, dynamic program so that, in God's time, they will choose to build a personal relationship with Him through Jesus Christ.

Thank you for your dedication and reflection on how to use your time, talents, and gifts to serve the children's ministry in the church. When you equip children with God's truth, you alone have this unique opportunity to witness their ever-growing love for God.

Please contact us to learn more about our ministry and how you can participate. Our Lighthouse Kids team will get in touch with you as soon as possible!

👶

Nursery (Birth–23 months)

An environment for babies where parents can still be part of the Sunday Worship through a telecast providing a livestream of the service to the Nursery Room. The nursery is a stimulating and fun environment for infants, crawlers and walkers. A space for nursing moms is provided in the Nursing Mom Room.

🌱

Early Childhood (Ages 2–5)

Children in this age group learn best through "hands-on" experience. With a dynamic and structured activity time, worship and Bible lesson, our time with the kids include crafts, snacks and playtime. Our desire is for children to receive the planting of the seed of Jesus Christ so their hearts would grow to know and love Jesus more and more!

📖

Late Childhood (Grades 1–6)

Creative and relevant curriculum is used to teach these growing kids the biblical truth in a fun and applicable way. The kids in Grades 1–6 begin their time with worship, an activity and prayer. We separate our age groups to meet the learning needs of each grade level. Our desire is for kids to leave knowing they are loved by Jesus Christ and their church family!
