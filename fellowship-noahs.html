諾亞團契

Noah's Fellowship

諾亞團契是一個以粵語為主的團契，主要目的是透過團契生活彼此建立，互相學習，在主的愛中互相鼓勵、代禱和服事。

Noah Fellowship is a Cantonese-language fellowship that primarily aims to build up and learn from one another through fellowship life, encouraging, interceding, and serving one another in His love.

♥ 聚會時間：每月第三個主日下午三時三十分 ♥

♥ Meeting Time: 3:30 PM on the third Sunday of each month ♥
