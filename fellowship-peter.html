彼得團契

Peter Fellowship

「你喂養我的羊。」— 約翰福音 21:17

「Feed my sheep.」

— John 21:17

我們的團契以使徒彼得命名，希望大家能同心合意，效法彼得的榜樣，愛主愛人，殷勤做主工，榮神益人。

Our fellowship is named after the Apostle Peter, and we hope that everyone will work together to learn from Peter's example, love the Lord and others, diligently do the Lord's work, and glorify God and benefit others.

團契成員大多是來自廣東和香港、在美國生活多年的海外華人，聚會主要以粵語進行。成員包括商業人士、各行各業的專業人士，以及退休的祖父母。聚會以查經和心得分享的形式進行，成員分享信仰和生命經歷，互相激勵，同走信仰之路。

The fellowship members are mostly overseas Chinese from Guangdong and Hong Kong who have lived in the United States for many years, and the meetings are primarily conducted in Cantonese. Members include business owners, professionals from various industries, and even retired grandparents. The meetings take the form of Bible study and sharing of insights, where members share their faith and life experiences, encouraging one another to walk the path of faith together.

因此，我們誠摯邀請新朋友和老朋友一同參與。

Therefore, we sincerely invite both new and old friends to participate.

♥ 聚會時間：每月第三個主日下午 ♥

♥ Meeting Time: The third Sunday afternoon of each month ♥
