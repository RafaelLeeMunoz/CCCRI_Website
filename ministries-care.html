關懷探訪

Care & Visits

聖經教導我們彼此相愛。在教會牧者的支持下，關懷部的同工們多年來不懈地推動關懷事工。我們跟進初次到訪的新朋友，組成探訪小組關顧有特別需要的弟兄姊妹，舉辦關懷主題研討會，關心長者及愛心午餐的成員，安慰那些悲傷的人，也為那些喜慶的人送上祝福。

The Bible teaches us to love one another. With the support of the church pastors, the staff of the Care Department have tirelessly promoted care ministry for many years. We follow up with newcomers to the church, form visiting groups to care for brothers and sisters with special needs, organize seminars on care topics, care for the elderly and members of the Blessing Meal, comfort those in sorrow, and offer blessings to those celebrating joyous occasions.

「與哭泣的人同哭，與快樂的人同樂。」

"Weep with those who weep, and rejoice with those who rejoice."

願主在羅德島華人教會的關懷事工中得榮耀！

May the Lord be glorified in the care ministry of our Rhode Island Chinese Church!
